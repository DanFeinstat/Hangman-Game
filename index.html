Carmen Sandiego, the criminal mastermind of the Villains International League of Evil (V.I.L.E) is the most notorious thief the world has ever know.

It is your mission as newly minted ACME Slueths to find out where Sandiego is now and return her ill-gotten gains to the proper owners.

Carmen's gang pulled another caper, and it's up to you to solve the case! Press any letter key to guess a letter and try to determine where Sandiego is hiding her score. Be careful though, if you chase down too many leads she might catch on and move to a different city. Good luck, gumshoe!
